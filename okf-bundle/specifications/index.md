# Specifications

* [Product Overview](product-overview.md) - What Estatly is, the deliverable surfaces, and the standing constraints from the requirements brief.
* [Domain Model](domain-model.md) - Estatly's core entities — Society, Project, Member, Ownership, Employee, Vendor, Work Order, Invoice — and how they relate.
* [Governance & Roles](governance-and-roles.md) - Membership, management, Executive Committee roles, employees, and the permission boundaries between them.
* [Vendor Workflow](vendor-workflow.md) - The work-order lifecycle — issue, execution, invoicing, employee verification, and EC approval.
* [Finance & Compliance](finance-and-compliance.md) - The four books of account, scanned-document storage, Indian statutory compliance, GST, and Tally export.
* [Payments](payments.md) - The member-facing payment gateway — built on third-party payment software, accessible from the mobile app.
* [Document Management](document-management.md) - Society-, project-, and member-level document registry with metadata search — generalizing the brief's ledger-linked scanned-document storage.
* [Member Requests (Ticketing)](member-requests.md) - Member-raised tickets for finance, maintenance, and records queries — entity, lifecycle, routing, and surfaces.
* [Communication](communication.md) - Bulletin boards at society (EC) and project (PC) level, and direct member messaging via email and WhatsApp.
* [Asset Management](asset-management.md) - The asset registry — flats, plots, villas, dividend holdings — and role-based visibility across members, EC, PCs, and employees.
* [Meetings, Voting & Resolutions](meetings-and-voting.md) - In-app meetings, e-voting on resolutions at society and project level, EC and PC elections, and typed e-signing of resolutions.
* [Platforms](platforms.md) - The client surfaces — admin panel, landing site, iOS and Android mobile apps — and who uses which.
* [Development Process](development-process.md) - TDD mandate and the per-layer test toolchains every Estatly codebase follows.
